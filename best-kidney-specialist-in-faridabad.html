When to Consult a Nephrologist in Faridabad

Early detection and treatment of kidney problems can prevent serious complications. Here are some signs that indicate it’s time to see a nephrologist in Faridabad, such as Dr. Jitendra Kumar:

  • Swelling in the legs, ankles, or face that does not go away.
  • Changes in urination, such as frequent urination, blood in the urine, or decreased output.
  • Unexplained fatigue or weakness.
  • High blood pressure that is not easily controlled.
  • Pain or discomfort in the lower back or sides.
  • Family history of kidney disease.
