Understanding the Importance of Nephrology

Before delving into the quest for the best nephrologists, let’s grasp the significance of nephrology itself. Nephrology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of kidney-related ailments. Given the kidneys’ vital role in filtering waste and excess fluids from the blood, any dysfunction can significantly impact overall health.
