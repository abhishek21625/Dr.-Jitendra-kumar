About Me

Dr. Jitendra Kumar, a distinguished expert in the field of Nephrology and Kidney Transplant medicine. As the Chairman of the Department of Renal Sciences & Transplant Medicine and the Former President of the Delhi Nephrology Society, he is widely recognized as the best nephrologist in Faridabad.

Dr. Jitendra Kumar's extensive expertise was honed through his DM (Nephrology) training at the prestigious Sanjay Gandhi Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences in Lucknow. He currently practices at Accord Superspeciality Hospital in Faridabad, making him the best nephrologist in faridabad.

One of Dr. Jitendra's notable achievements is introducing advanced treatments such as CRRT, Hemoperfusion, CAPD, and Kidney Transplants in Faridabad for the first time. This has solidified his position as a pioneering figure in kidney healthcare. If you're seeking exceptional care and a leading kidney hospital in Faridabad, Dr. Jitendra Kumar at Accord Superspeciality Hospital should be your first choice.
